Banana Pancakes Recipe

Banana pancakes are a delightful twist on the classic breakfast favorite, offering a natural sweetness and a rich, moist texture. These pancakes are made by incorporating ripe bananas into the batter, which gives them a soft, fluffy consistency and a subtle, fruity flavor. The sweetness of the bananas eliminates the need for excess sugar, making them a healthier option that still satisfies your sweet tooth.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Batter: In a mixing bowl, mash 2 ripe bananas until smooth. Add 1 cup of flour, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 egg, 3/4 cup of milk, and a pinch of salt. Mix everything until smooth and well combined. If you like, add a splash of vanilla extract for extra flavor.

step 2

Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip the pancake and cook for another 2 minutes or until golden brown.

step 3

Serve and Enjoy: Stack the pancakes on a plate and top with your favorite toppings such as sliced bananas, maple syrup, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. Enjoy your homemade banana pancakes!

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk
